# 📦 Supply Chain Cost Optimization Analysis

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project analyzes supply chain logistics data from a fashion and beauty product dataset to identify cost inefficiencies across transportation routes and modes. The goal is to derive actionable insights and recommend strategies to optimize logistics cost and operational efficiency.

---

## 📊 Objective
- Identify cost drivers in supply chain logistics
- Compare transportation modes and route efficiency
- Evaluate trade-offs between cost and quality (defect rates)
- Conduct scenario analysis for cost optimization

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy) – Data analysis & aggregation  
- Tableau – Data visualization & dashboard creation  
- Excel – Data inspection & preprocessing  

---

## 📁 Dataset Features
- Product Type, SKU, Price, Revenue  
- Transportation Modes (Air, Sea, Rail, Road)  
- Routes (A, B, C)  
- Costs, Shipping Time, Shipping Cost  
- Defect Rates, Lead Time, Stock Levels  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed
- Cost analysis by **Route**
- Cost analysis by **Transportation Mode**
- Combined analysis (Route × Mode interaction)
- Defect rate vs Cost trade-off analysis
- Scenario simulation for cost optimization

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Route B consistently shows the highest logistics cost across all transportation modes  
- Sea transport is the most cost-efficient option overall  
- Air transport provides faster delivery but significantly higher cost  
- Road transport shows relatively high defect rates without clear cost advantage  

---

## 📉 Scenario Analysis Results

### Scenario 1: Route Optimization
- Reducing dependency on Route B leads to **~8.3% cost reduction**

### Scenario 2: Mode Optimization
- Shifting from Air to Sea results in **~6.9% cost reduction**

### Combined Impact
- Total potential cost reduction exceeds **~15%**

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

- Reduce dependency on Route B due to consistently high costs  
- Use Sea transport as the default mode for non-urgent shipments  
- Limit Air transport to high-priority or time-sensitive deliveries  
- Investigate inefficiencies in Route B infrastructure and operations  
- Optimize transportation mix to balance cost and delivery time
